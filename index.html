Our Story

One Concept.
Then Everything Else.

"We didn't set out to build a restaurant group. We set out to build something great. The group followed."

Saddleback Restaurant Group started with a single BBQ concept and a relentless focus on quality. Over more than a decade, that obsession with craft—and a disciplined approach to operations—laid the foundation for something bigger.

Today, SRG operates four distinct brands across greater Lansing, each with its own identity and audience. What unifies them is the same standard: great food, excellent execution, and a culture of continuous improvement.

Origin
Saddleback BBQ founded in the Lansing area
Expansion
Second Saddleback BBQ location opened; Slice by Saddleback launched
Acquisition
Coach's Pub & Grill acquired and integrated into the SRG portfolio
Next
Assembly opens in downtown Mason — the group's most ambitious concept yet
